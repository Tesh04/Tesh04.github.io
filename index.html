Will Arsenal finally take the crown or will the Man City Blues halt their dreams again

Read time: 15 minutes

As the 2024/25 Premier League season kicks off, the excitement over title contenders and relegation battles is at its peak. Powerhouses like Manchester City and Arsenal are widely considered the frontrunners for the title. City, led by Pep Guardiola, will likely remain dominant with a squad stacked with world-class talent, including Erling Haaland, Kevin De Bruyne, and Phil Foden. Arsenal, with their youthful resurgence under Mikel Arteta, could push City to the wire, buoyed by stars like Bukayo Saka and Martin Ødegaard. Liverpool and Manchester United are expected to be in the title mix as well. With Jurgen Klopp’s reinvigorated squad and Erik ten Hag’s defensive solidity, both teams could pose serious threats to the title chase. Chelsea and Tottenham, after periods of rebuilding, will aim for Champions League spots but may fall short of a title challenge. At the other end of the table, newly promoted sides like **Southampton**, **Leeds United**, and **Leicester City** are predicted to face a tough relegation fight. Despite their experience in the Premier League, these clubs will need to adapt quickly to survive. Southampton, after a season in the Championship, might find it challenging to adjust, while Leeds and Leicester, having narrowly missed survival in the past, will need significant squad improvements. Other clubs, such as Bournemouth and Wolverhampton, might also find themselves dragged into the relegation scrap if they fail to strengthen their squads. Ultimately, Manchester City remains the favorite to win the 2024/25 Premier League, while Southampton, Leeds United, and Leicester City are tipped as potential relegation candidates.
